How We Work

A Proven Design Process Built for Sport

We combine KPMG's consulting rigour with a deeply human-centred design methodology to deliver outcomes — not just outputs.

1

Discover & Research

We start by listening. Stakeholder interviews, fan surveys, analytics audits and competitive benchmarking give us a complete picture of where you are and where the opportunities lie.

2

Define & Align

We synthesise research into clear problem statements, strategic objectives and design principles — creating alignment across your leadership, technology and commercial teams.

3

Design & Prototype

Rapid concept development, wireframing and high-fidelity prototyping. We test early and iterate fast, validating with real users before any development begins.

4

Build & Deliver

Design handoff to engineering, front-end development and quality assurance — all under one roof. We work alongside your existing tech teams or deliver end-to-end.

5

Measure & Optimise

Post-launch analytics, usability reviews and continuous improvement cycles ensure your digital products keep performing as your audiences evolve.

Why Design Matters in Sport

The organisations winning the digital race in sport aren't the ones with the biggest budgets — they're the ones who truly understand their audiences and design every interaction with intention.

  • ✓ Human-centred design increases fan retention by making every digital moment feel effortless and personal
  • ✓ Strategic UX removes friction from ticketing, membership and e-commerce — directly growing revenue
  • ✓ Data-led optimisation turns gut-feel decisions into evidence-based ones that compound over time
  • ✓ A consistent design system reduces build costs and speeds up time-to-market for new digital products
  • ✓ Inclusive, accessible design expands your addressable audience and demonstrates social responsibility
